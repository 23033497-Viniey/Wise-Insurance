How to Purchase Insurance with WiseInsurance

Step 1: Assess Your Needs

Before purchasing insurance, consider:

  • Your current health status
  • Family medical history
  • Lifestyle factors (e.g., smoking, exercise habits)
  • Budget for monthly premiums
Tip: Make a list of your priorities. Do you need comprehensive coverage or are you looking for basic protection?

Step 2: Compare Our Plans

WiseInsurance offers three main plans:

  • Basic Plan ($50/month): Essential coverage for common health issues
  • Standard Plan ($100/month): Broader coverage including specialist consultations
  • Premium Plan ($200/month): Comprehensive coverage with additional benefits like dental and vision
Tip: Consider your long-term needs. A slightly higher premium might save you money in the long run.

Step 3: Get a Quote

To get an accurate quote:

  1. Visit our online quote tool or call our helpline
  2. Provide basic information about yourself
  3. Answer health-related questions honestly
  4. Review the quoted premiums for each plan
Tip: Be prepared with your medical history and current medications list when getting a quote.

Step 4: Apply for Coverage

Once you've chosen a plan:

  1. Fill out the application form (online or with an agent)
  2. Provide necessary documentation (e.g., ID, proof of address)
  3. Complete a medical questionnaire or examination if required
  4. Review and sign the policy documents
Tip: Read the policy documents carefully. Don't hesitate to ask questions about anything you don't understand.

Step 5: Activate Your Policy

To activate your chosen policy:

  1. Set up your payment method (usually direct debit or credit card)
  2. Make your first premium payment
  3. Receive your policy documents and insurance card
  4. Note the start date of your coverage
Tip: Mark your renewal date on your calendar. Reviewing your policy annually ensures it still meets your needs.
